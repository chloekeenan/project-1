Three Day Itinerary:


Day One

Once you've parked for the weekend, you can stop driving altogether! No one will bother you as you meditate or self-practice in the yoga studio, stroll to the beach, or take a nap.
Now is the time to book your weekend massage. Enjoy a delicious vegetarian supper while getting to know your fellow yogis.
A short meditation will be followed by a gentle practice to help you recover from your travel day.


Day Two

A light breakfast and a yoga session with optional meditation will kick off your day at 7 a.m., after which you'll have some free time till lunch.
We'll go on a stroll in the rain forest after lunch, followed by a sea swim. Before your dinner reservation at 7 o'clock, you can visit our spa for a massage and facial.
On the beach, there will be a cocktail-making workshop following dinner.



Day Three

Sunrise yoga and a sea swim will be our morning activities.
After that, we'll have a brunch to say goodbye to everyone before journaling about the retreat. departure at 3 p.m.
